Editing Techniques

A quick breakdown of techniques I use to improve photos/videos.

Color correction

Correct exposure and white balance first. Then adjust contrast/saturation to match the mood.

Effects

Effects should support the story: glow, blur, film grain, or subtle light leaks.

Transitions

A clean transition often comes from timing: cut on movement, match framing, then add motion blur.

Audio syncing

TikTok edits feel “professional” when cuts land on beats and the audio is balanced.
